<p><i>Quick Timer Tile</i> makes managing your time effortless and distraction-free.</p><p>Add it to your Quick Settings, and you’re ready to go – no app icon or traditional interface. Everything happens through a timer dialog and notification.</p><p>How to get started:</p><ol><li>Add the Timer to Quick Settings:<ul><li>Swipe down from the top of your screen to open Quick Settings.</li><li>Tap the pencil icon or "Edit" to customize your tiles.</li><li>Drag the "Timer" tile into the active area.</li></ul></li><li>Set up your timer:<ul><li>Tap the "Timer" tile to open the timer setup dialog.</li><li>Grant notifications permission (if needed).</li><li>Use the pickers to set the desired time and press "Start".</li></ul></li><li>Follow the Timer in Notifications:<ul><li>Once the timer starts, a notification shows the remaining time.</li><li>Pause, resume, or cancel the timer directly from the notification with one tap.</li></ul></li></ol><p>Why use Quick Timer?</p><ul><li>Quick access: start a timer in seconds straight from Quick Settings.</li><li>No clutter: no app screen or icon – just a clean, efficient experience.</li><li>Convenient notification: always know how much time is left at a glance.</li></ul><p>Perfect for cooking, workouts, or any activity where timing matters!</p>
